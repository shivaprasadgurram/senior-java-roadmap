Fast Track: 1-Month Immediate Interview Plan

(~18 hours/week)

This accelerated 1-month plan is designed for immediate interviews, focusing on the most critical and frequently asked topics in Java, Spring Boot, Microservices, LLD, and essential DSA. It prioritizes breadth over depth, ensuring you're ready to discuss foundational and intermediate concepts and solve common coding challenges under time pressure. This plan assumes you start tomorrow for 30 days!

Phase 1: Foundation & Pattern Recognition

(Months 1-2)

This initial phase is about building a rock-solid foundation for your preparation. You will solidify fundamental DSA concepts, begin to recognize common problem-solving patterns, and get introduced to the core principles of System Design and AI integration. With a commitment of ~18 hours per week (10 hours weekdays, 8 hours weekends), this phase lays the groundwork for success. Consistency is key here.
